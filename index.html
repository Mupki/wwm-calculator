Community Findings

What the Math
Actually Shows

A few things discovered through testing that aren't documented anywhere in EN — or are documented wrong.

Healing Variance
The Heal Range is ±7.5%, Not ±10%
The CN wiki and most EN guides state a ±10% healing fluctuation. In-game testing on the EN client consistently shows ±7.5%. Small difference, but it affects heal range predictions.
EN-Tested · Differs from CN Wiki
Agility Scaling
Agility is Double-Value for Deluge Until 200
A single Agility point simultaneously increases Min Physical Attack (via Umbrella passive) and Critical Rate (via Fan passive). Both passives cap at 200 Agility. Below that threshold, Agility is effectively two stats in one.
Confirmed · Formula Verified
Penetration Formula
Breaking Enemy Resistance Doubles Your Pen Value
When your penetration exceeds enemy resistance, the excess is divided by 100. Below enemy resistance, it's divided by 200. This creates a breakpoint where pen suddenly becomes twice as efficient — and most players don't know it exists.
Confirmed · Matches CN Community Testing
Min/Max Breakpoint
Exceeding Max Attack Collapses Your Roll Range
The game confirms this in the tooltip itself: if Min Attribute Attack exceeds Max, every hit deals a fixed amount equal to Min. Every point past the breakpoint is then double-valued. For off-path attributes starting at zero, this threshold is crossed immediately.
Confirmed · In-Game Tooltip Verified
Soulshade Umbrella Q
It Doesn't Give Shields — That's a CN-Only Thing
Multiple CN resources describe the Umbrella Q as providing shields. In the EN version it heals and grants a 20% damage buff for 12 seconds on a 30-second cooldown. There's also a passive: +5% damage on exhausted enemies while the buff is active.
EN-Corrected · CN Sources Wrong
Physical Min Breakpoint
The Physical Min Breakpoint is Unreachable at Current Gear
With 12 total tuning slots for Min Physical at 54.8 max each, the theoretical maximum from gear alone is ~657 — just short of the ~660 gap at current gear levels. Not worth chasing. Focus on Crit Rate instead.
Confirmed · Gear Math Verified
