Step 1 — Install the Gear Fetcher Bookmarklet
A bookmarklet runs on the official game site — bypassing CORS entirely — grabs your character data, and copies it to your clipboard in one click. Drag the button below to your bookmarks bar.

WWM Gear Fetch ← drag this to your bookmarks bar

Paste below and click Parse
